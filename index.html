How It Works & Your Privacy Guarantee

This tool uses the powerful jsdiff library to compare texts directly on your device. When you paste your text and click 'Compare', our code runs locally in your browser. Your data never leaves your computer and is never sent to our servers. This makes it the perfect tool for comparing sensitive documents, code, or private notes.

Use Cases:

  • Writers & Editors: Track changes between different drafts of an article or book.
  • Developers: Quickly check differences in code snippets without using command-line tools.
  • Legal Professionals: Compare contract versions to spot subtle modifications.
  • Students: Review revisions made to your essays or reports.
